Avatar: The Last Airbender

Avatar: The Last Airbender is a series produced by Netflix in partnership with Nickelodeon, aiming to retell the story of Avatar: The Last Airbender in a live-action format. Avatar creators Michael Dante DiMartino and Bryan Konietzko were originally set to return to helm the adaptation, but announced their departure from the series in 2020 citing creative differences with Netflix. On March 15, 2021, Albert Kim, who had worked with Mike and Bryan on the show prior to their departure, was announced as the series' sole showrunner.

The first episode debuted on February 15, 2024 in Los Angeles as part of the red carpet premiere of the series, with the full streaming release on Netflix a week later on February 22. The first season consists of eight episodes.

On March 6, 2024, it was announced that the live-action series had been renewed for two more seasons, covering Books Two and Three. On April 4, 2024, it was announced that Albert Kim would step down as showrunner for seasons two and three, though will continue as an executive producer. He will be replaced as showrunner by Christine Boylan, who had previously worked as a writer on the first season, together with director and VFX supervisor Jabbar Raisani. On June 11, 2024, it was announced that shooting for Season Two is anticipated to begin in Fall 2024.

Cast

Aang

Gordon Cormier as

Aang

Aang is the last surviving Air Nomad and airbender. As the Avatar, he is also the only person in the world who is capable of manipulating all four elements. An airbending prodigy, Aang struggles to deal with the burden of his duties while still holding on to his adventurous and playful nature.

Katara

Kiawentiio as

Katara

Katara is a waterbender from the Southern Water Tribe and the daughter of Hakoda and Kya. The last remaining waterbender in the small village of Wolf Cove, after she and her older brother, Sokka, discover Aang trapped in an iceberg, they decide to accompany him on his journey across the world.

Sokka

Ian Ousley as

Sokka

Sokka is a warrior in the Southern Water Tribe, and the temporary leader of the small village of Wolf Cove. The son of Hakoda and Kya, after he and his younger sister, Katara, discover Aang trapped in an iceberg, they decide to accompany him on his journey across the world.

Zuko

Dallas Liu as

Zuko

Zuko is the Crown Prince of the Fire Nation, the son of Fire Lord Ozai, and older brother of Princess Azula. A skilled firebender, Zuko was gravely wounded during an Agni Kai against his father and subsequently exiled from the Fire Nation. He then set out on a quest to hunt down and capture the Avatar accompanied by his uncle, General Iroh, believing it is the only way he can reclaim his honor and return home.

Iroh

Paul Sun-Hyung Lee as

Iroh

Iroh is a retired Fire Nation general and the brother of Fire Lord Ozai. After Prince Zuko was exiled from the Fire Nation, Iroh accompanied him on his quest to hunt down and capture the Avatar, acting as his hot-headed nephew's nurturing mentor.

Zhao

Ken Leung as

Zhao

Zhao was a commander of the Fire Nation, who commanded a Navy Vessel and wanted was to capture the Avatar and to kill the Moon Spirit in the Northern Water Tribe in order to make a name for himself. His true ambition was to become Fire Lord, attempting to sway Iroh to back his claim.

Ozai

Daniel Dae Kim as

Ozai

Ozai is the current Fire Lord of the Fire Nation, and father to Prince Zuko and Princess Azula. A highly accomplished firebender and ruthlessly-driven leader, Ozai considers it his destiny to unite the world under Fire Nation rule and finish the century-long war started by his grandfather, Fire Lord Sozin.
